Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and clicking on the order details. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order is shipped.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 15 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with tags attached.

How can I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team 24/7 through our live chat, email at support@liestyle.com, or by calling (+91) 6306407861.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination.
